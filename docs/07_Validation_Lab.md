# Validation Lab

## Purpose

The Validation Lab asks whether OBRM risk bands have historically separated more favourable and less favourable future-return environments.

## No-look-ahead boundary

1. Calculate historical OBRM analytics using only information available at each date.
2. Freeze those risk and confidence values.
3. Calculate future returns afterward.
4. Group outcomes by the original historical risk band.

## Current horizons

- 90 days
- 365 days
- 730 days

## Core outputs

- Observations per risk band
- Median and mean forward return
- Positive-outcome percentage
- Worst and best outcomes
- Negative 365-day outcome frequency
- Monotonicity score
- Cycle-era stability

This is research evidence, not proof or a guarantee.
