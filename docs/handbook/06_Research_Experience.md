# Research Handbook — Research Experience

## Progressive disclosure

OBRM separates information into three levels.

### Executive

Shows:

- Bitcoin price
- Composite Risk
- Model Confidence
- current decision
- historical chart

### Analyst

Adds:

- Market Dimension cards
- weighted contributions
- Primary Driver
- Model Confidence Audit
- decision explanation

### Research

Adds:

- raw values
- calculation precision
- dimension weights
- weighted contributions
- fair-value premium
- momentum inputs
- volatility inputs
- model methodology

## Historical Research Snapshot

The native Plotly hover displays the model's historical state for each date without changing the saved model values.

It includes:

- price
- fair value
- premium or discount
- Market Dimension scores
- Composite Risk
- Model Confidence
- market phase
- decision
- DCA settings
- source
