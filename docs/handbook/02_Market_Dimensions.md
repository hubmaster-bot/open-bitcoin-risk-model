# Research Handbook — Market Dimensions

## Why Market Dimensions?

Valuation, momentum, and volatility answer different questions. They are not interchangeable indicators and do not measure one identical form of risk.

## Valuation

**Question:** Is Bitcoin expensive or attractive relative to long-term fair value?

## Momentum

**Question:** How strongly is the market moving?

## Volatility

**Question:** How stable or turbulent are market conditions?

## Composite Risk

Each Market Dimension is normalized to 0–1 and weighted:

- Valuation: 45%
- Momentum: 30%
- Volatility: 25%

The weights are provisional and must continue to be validated.
