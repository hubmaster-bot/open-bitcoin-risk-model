# Research Handbook — Composite Risk

Composite Risk combines normalized Market Dimensions using visible weights.

Current weights:

- Valuation: 45%
- Momentum: 30%
- Volatility: 25%

For each dimension:

`Contribution = Dimension Score × Weight`

Composite Risk is the sum of those contributions.

Example:

- Valuation: 0.80 × 45% = 0.360
- Momentum: 0.60 × 30% = 0.180
- Volatility: 0.40 × 25% = 0.100

Composite Risk = 0.640

The largest weighted contribution is reported as the Primary Driver.
