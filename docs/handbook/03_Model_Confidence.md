# Research Handbook — Model Confidence

## Purpose

Composite Risk describes what the model thinks.

Model Confidence describes how much trust to place in that assessment.

## Coverage

Measures how many expected Market Dimensions are available.

## Agreement

Measures how closely the Market Dimensions align.

## Data Quality

Measures whether the underlying inputs are complete, current, and valid.

## Model Maturity

Measures regression fit and historical sample maturity.

## Important distinction

Model Confidence is not a probability that Bitcoin will rise or fall.
