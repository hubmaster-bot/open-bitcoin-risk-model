# Research Handbook — On-Chain Promotion Review

## Purpose

The promotion review is the governance boundary between research evidence and
the live OBRM model.

## Possible outcomes

- **Promote:** the provisional gate passed, but implementation still requires a
  separate release.
- **Research only:** evidence is promising but incomplete.
- **Not ready:** one or more critical criteria failed.

## Why promotion is separate

Changing Composite Risk affects:

- historical scores,
- model confidence,
- decisions,
- portfolio guidance,
- backtests,
- and user interpretation.

A candidate therefore cannot enter the live model merely because one validation
page looks encouraging.

## Provider gaps

The current on-chain research layer still lacks:

- Holder Behaviour
- Realized Capital Flow

These may later come from another free provider or from self-computed Bitcoin
Core metrics.
