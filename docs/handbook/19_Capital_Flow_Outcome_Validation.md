# Capital Flow Outcome Validation

v0.10.5 asks whether the Capital Flow Research Score is useful, not merely reproducible. Stronger scores should separate future return and drawdown outcomes across several horizons, remain stable in later chronological windows, and behave consistently across independent providers.

The review returns **Promote**, **Research Only**, or **Not Ready**. Promote means the evidence is eligible for a separately designed Composite Risk v2; it does not alter the current model.
