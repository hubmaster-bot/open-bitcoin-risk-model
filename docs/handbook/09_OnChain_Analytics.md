# Research Handbook — On-Chain Analytics

## MVRV

MVRV compares Bitcoin's market value with realized value.

It helps describe whether market valuation is low or elevated relative to the aggregate cost basis represented by realized capitalization.

## Realized Capitalization

Realized capitalization values each coin at the price when it last moved rather than today's market price.

OBRM measures its 365-day growth as a transparent capital-flow proxy.

## Active Supply

One-year active supply is optional in v0.9.1 and is included only when the Community catalogue confirms availability.

## Normalization

Each input is converted to an expanding percentile using only information available on or before each date.

## Diagnostic score

The v0.9.1 On-Chain Research Score is an equal-weight average of available normalized inputs, smoothed over 30 days.

It is not yet an official Market Dimension.
