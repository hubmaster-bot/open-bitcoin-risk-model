# User Guide — Portfolio Lab

## Purpose

The Portfolio Lab translates a generic market decision into a portfolio-aware planning example.

## Inputs

- BTC holdings
- Cash available
- Normal weekly DCA
- Total contributed capital
- Target BTC allocation
- Permanent BTC core
- Optional capital-recovery limit

## Safeguards

- Purchases cannot exceed available cash.
- Sales cannot reduce holdings below the permanent core.
- Low-confidence signals cannot trigger an active action.
- Capital recovery can limit sales to unrecovered contributions.

## Important

The Portfolio Lab does not model:

- income,
- debt,
- emergency funds,
- tax,
- fees,
- jurisdiction,
- personal risk tolerance,
- or suitability.
