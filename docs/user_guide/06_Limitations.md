# User Guide — Limitations and Safety

OBRM remains a research project.

Important limitations include:

- Few independent Bitcoin market cycles
- Overlapping forward-return windows
- Provisional weights and thresholds
- No transaction-cost model
- No tax model
- No on-chain dimension yet
- No macro dimension yet
- Historical relationships may fail
- Market structure may change
- Data providers may contain errors
- Execution may differ from the model

Never treat one score or recommendation as sufficient evidence for a major financial decision.
