# User Guide — Understanding Recommendations

## Generic decision

The Decision Engine converts Composite Risk into a generic action.

Current research-alpha actions include:

- Aggressive accumulation
- Accumulation
- Normal DCA
- Reduced DCA
- Pause new DCA
- Gradual DCA out
- Stronger DCA out

## Decision Audit

The Decision Audit shows:

- all Market Dimension scores,
- standard labels,
- visible weights,
- weighted contributions,
- Composite Risk,
- Model Confidence,
- Primary Driver,
- and the resulting decision.

## Primary Driver

The Primary Driver is the Market Dimension with the largest weighted contribution to Composite Risk.

## DCA out

DCA-out recommendations remain research-alpha outputs.

They are not validated for:

- taxes,
- fees,
- spread,
- slippage,
- legal constraints,
- personal circumstances,
- or emotional execution.
