# User Guide — Getting Started

## What OBRM is

OBRM is an open-source research framework for understanding Bitcoin market conditions.

It combines:

- historical Bitcoin price data,
- long-term fair-value analysis,
- Market Dimensions,
- Composite Risk,
- Model Confidence,
- decision support,
- portfolio planning,
- backtesting,
- and validation.

## What OBRM is not

OBRM is not:

- a guaranteed trading system,
- a price predictor,
- personalised financial advice,
- tax advice,
- legal advice,
- or a substitute for independent judgement.

## Standard workflow

1. Refresh the market data.
2. Review the Dashboard.
3. Open Model Intelligence for the Market Dimensions.
4. Open Decision Intelligence to understand the recommendation.
5. Use Portfolio Lab only after entering realistic constraints.
6. Use Backtesting and Validation to understand historical behaviour.
